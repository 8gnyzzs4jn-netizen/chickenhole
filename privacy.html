Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 10, 2026

1. Data Collection

Chicken Hole does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. All content you store in the app — including photos, videos, audio recordings, notes, and passwords — is saved exclusively on your device.

2. Local Storage Only

No data ever leaves your device. There are no servers, no cloud sync, and no user accounts. Your vault content is stored in the app's sandboxed local storage.

3. Encryption

Private notes are encrypted using AES-GCM 256-bit encryption. Encryption keys are generated on your device and never transmitted anywhere.

4. Camera Access

The app requests camera access only when you choose to add photos/videos to your vault, or when the Intruder Selfie feature (premium) is enabled. No images are transmitted externally.

5. Purchases

In-app purchases are processed by Apple via StoreKit. We do not receive or store any payment information.

6. Analytics

We do not use any analytics tools, tracking SDKs, or third-party services.

7. Children

This app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Contact

Questions? Email us at app.creator@icloud.com
