FAQ

I forgot my PIN or sequence

For your security, there is no PIN recovery. If you forget your PIN or sequence, you will need to reinstall the app. All vault content will be lost. Please store your credentials safely.

How do I unlock the vault?

In the game, tap the holes in the secret sequence you set during setup, then enter your 4-digit PIN when prompted.

How do I restore my purchase?

Go to Settings → Premium → Restore Purchases.

Is my data backed up?

Your vault content is stored locally on your device. If you use iCloud Backup, the app data may be included in your device backup. We recommend using Wi-Fi Transfer (premium) to keep copies of your important files.

What is the Intruder Selfie feature?

When enabled (premium), the app automatically takes a photo using the front camera when someone enters the wrong PIN 3 times. The photo is saved in your vault under Settings → Intruder Log.
